Legal Terms of use and Data privacy

A terms and conditions agreement outlines the rules around user data and provides information about the actions the website will execute. The terms and conditions content is a legal contract between website and its users. In the event of a violation of contract conditions, adjudicators will look at it to determine whether each petitioner behaved within their rights.
